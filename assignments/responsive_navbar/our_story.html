Our Story

Our story began with a passion for cats and a desire to make a difference in our community. We saw the need to provide a temporary home for cats and kittens that were at risk of being euthanized due to overcrowding in local shelters. Our cafe is a unique and innovative way to give these cats the chance to socialize, play, and receive love and attention from our visitors, while they wait for their forever homes.

We take pride in partnering with local shelters and animal rescue organizations to help reduce euthanasia rates and find loving homes for our cafe cats. Our cafe is the perfect place for cat lovers and students from the nearby University of Central Florida to relax, study, and enjoy our friendly feline residents. We offer a welcoming and chill atmosphere, delicious food, and coffee, and an opportunity to make a difference in the lives of homeless cats. Come visit us at Calico Cafe and join us in our mission to provide a better life for cats in need.
