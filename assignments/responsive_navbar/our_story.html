Our Story

Welcome to Calico Cafe, the purr-fect place for cat lovers! Our story began with two best friends, Sarah and Emily, who had a passion for cats and dreamed of opening a cafe. They wanted to create a space where people could enjoy a cup of coffee or a delicious pastry while also spending time with adorable cats. Their dream became a reality in 2018 when Calico Cafe opened its doors near the University of Central Florida.

At Calico Cafe, we believe in giving back to our community. That's why we partner with local shelters to rescue cats at risk of euthanasia and give them a second chance at finding their forever home. Our adoption process is carefully designed to ensure that every cat finds a loving family who will provide them with the care and attention they deserve. We take pride in being a part of the solution to cat overpopulation and homelessness.

Our cafe is more than just a place to grab a coffee; it's an experience. Our cozy atmosphere and playful feline friends create a unique and memorable setting. We offer a variety of drinks and snacks, including vegan and gluten-free options, so there's something for everyone. We also host events such as cat yoga and movie nights, so be sure to check our calendar for upcoming activities. We hope you enjoy your visit to Calico Cafe and leave with a warm heart and a smile on your face.
