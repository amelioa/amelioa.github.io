Our Mission

At Calico Cafe, our mission is to provide a safe and loving temporary home for cats at risk of euthanasia and to promote animal welfare and responsible pet ownership. We strive to reduce euthanasia rates by helping these rescued cats find loving homes and to educate our community on the importance of spaying/neutering, adoption, and responsible pet care.

We believe that cats are valuable members of our community and deserve to be treated with kindness and respect. Our cafe provides a unique and innovative way to promote animal welfare and help cats in need, while also offering a welcoming and chill atmosphere for our visitors. We are committed to partnering with local shelters and animal rescue organizations to make a positive impact on the lives of homeless cats and to promote a more compassionate and responsible society.

Our goal is to create a community of cat lovers and advocates who share our passion for animal welfare and who are dedicated to making a difference in the lives of animals in need. We believe that by working together, we can reduce euthanasia rates and create a better world for cats and all animals. Thank you for supporting our mission at Calico Cafe!
