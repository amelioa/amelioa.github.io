Our Mission

At Calico Cafe, our mission is to create a safe and loving environment for cats and help them find their forever homes. We believe that every cat deserves a chance at life, and we strive to reduce the number of cats at risk of euthanasia by partnering with local shelters and rescuing cats in need. Our goal is to provide a second chance at life to as many cats as possible and to spread awareness about responsible pet ownership.

We achieve our mission by incorporating cat rescue into our business model. By partnering with local shelters, we are able to take in cats who are at risk of being euthanized and provide them with a temporary home until they find their forever family. Our cats live in a free-roaming environment, where they are socialized and cared for by our team of cat-loving staff members. We also provide veterinary care and ensure that every cat is spayed or neutered before being adopted.

We are dedicated to promoting responsible pet ownership and educating our community about the importance of adopting cats from shelters. We work closely with our adoption partners to match our cats with the right families and provide support to new pet owners. Our goal is not only to save as many cats as possible but also to create a community of cat lovers who share our passion for animal welfare. We hope to inspire others to make a difference in the lives of cats and to spread awareness about the importance of animal rescue.
