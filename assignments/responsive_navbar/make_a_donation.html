Make a Donation

Thank you for considering a donation to Calico Cafe! Your contribution will help us to rescue and care for cats in need and to promote responsible pet ownership. Here's how to donate:

  1. Online Donation: You can make a donation online through our website. Simply visit our donation page and select the amount you would like to donate. You can choose to make a one-time donation or set up a recurring donation.
  2. In-person Donation: You can also make a donation in person at our cafe. Simply let one of our staff members know that you would like to make a donation, and they will assist you in the process.
  3. Donation by Mail: If you prefer to make a donation by mail, you can send a check or money order to our mailing address. Please make sure to include your name and contact information so that we can send you a receipt for your donation.
  4. Amazon Wish List: Another way to donate is by purchasing items from our Amazon Wish List. These items are essential for our cats' well-being and include things like food, litter, and toys. Simply visit our Amazon Wish List page, select the items you would like to donate, and have them shipped directly to us.

We are a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, so your donation is tax-deductible. If you would like to receive a receipt for your donation, please make sure to provide your name and contact information. Thank you for your support of Calico Cafe and our mission to rescue and care for cats in need!
