About Me

Hello, my name is Alyssa! I am a dedicated graphic design and web development student based in Vero Beach, FL studying at the Florida Institute of Recording, Sound, and Technology.

I have always had an eye for design and a knack for technology. Finding my passion for graphic design and web development has been nothing short of amazing. My passion drives me to expand my knowledge and refine my skills in both disciplines everyday.

I will be graduating from F.I.R.S.T. Institute August 2023 and I am eager to dive into the graphic design and web development industry. I am hungry to achieve my goal of being a successful designer. There is nothing more satisfying than bringing a vision to life and I am incredibly excited to be able to do that as a career!
