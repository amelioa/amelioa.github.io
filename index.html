About Me

Hello, my name is Alyssa! I am a dedicated graphic designer and web developer based in Vero Beach. I recently graduated from the Florida Institute of Recording, Sound, and Technology with a diploma in Graphic Design and Web Development.

I have always had an eye for design and a knack for technology. Finding my passion for graphic design and web development has been nothing short of amazing. My passion drives me to expand my knowledge and refine my skills in both disciplines everyday.

I am eager to dive into the graphic design and web development industry. I am hungry to achieve my goal of being a successful designer. There is nothing more satisfying than bringing a vision to life and I am incredibly excited to be able to do that as a career!
